Supplements for Geometric Control of Mechanical Systems

These chapters cover material not in the text, or extend material that is in the text. Some notes.
  • These supplementary chapters are not intended to be self-contained, except when concatenated with the material in the book.
  • Some items in the index in the supplementary material may be cross-referenced to the index of the text.
  • These chapters are dynamic. That is to say, there is no ``official'' version. Thus they may well change from time to time, so keep track of the date. If things become sufficiently static in the future, we may call some version ``official,'' and then make errata for it to keep track of changes.
  • To refer to these supplements, here is a BiBTeX entry. 
    @Misc{FB/ADL:04supp,
  author =       {Francesco Bullo and Andrew D. Lewis},
  title =        {Supplementary Chapters for \emph{{G}eometric {C}ontrol of
                  {M}echanical {S}ystems}~\cite{FB/ADL:04}},
  year =         2005,
  month =        jan,
  url =          {http://motion.mee.ucsb.edu/book-gcms/}
}
    where FB/ADL:04 is the label for the book. The year and month should be changed to match whatever is the date on the front page of the supplementary chapters.
